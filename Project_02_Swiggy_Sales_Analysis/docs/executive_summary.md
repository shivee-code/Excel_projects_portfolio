# Executive Summary — Swiggy Sales & Customer Behavior Analysis

This analysis evaluates Swiggy’s sales performance and customer behavior using order-level data to identify revenue trends, customer satisfaction patterns, food preferences, and regional performance.

### Key Business Findings:

- **Strong Revenue Performance:**  
  The platform generated significant overall sales with consistent order volume, indicating stable demand across regions.

- **Customer Satisfaction:**  
  The average customer rating remains consistently high, reflecting a positive user experience and service quality.

- **Food Preference Trends:**  
  Non-Veg food contributes a larger share of revenue compared to Veg items, highlighting customer preference patterns and pricing impact.

- **Geographic Insights:**  
  A small group of metropolitan cities contributes a major portion of total sales, emphasizing the importance of urban markets for revenue growth.

- **Time-Based Trends:**  
  Sales show noticeable variation across days of the week and weeks of the year, helping identify peak demand periods for operational planning.

### Business Impact:
These insights can support:
- Targeted marketing campaigns in high-performing cities
- Menu optimization based on food preference trends
- Resource allocation during peak demand periods
- Performance tracking using KPI-driven dashboards

Overall, the dashboard enables stakeholders to monitor performance, identify growth opportunities, and make informed data-driven decisions.
